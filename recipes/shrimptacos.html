How To Make Shrimp Tacos

Description

These shrimp tacos won second best at the World Shrimp Taco Festival.

You won't believe how easy they are to make yourself! Smash that bell, baby.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of frozen shrimps (or get some fresh, Sailormoon!)
  • Some tortillas
  • Cole Slaw fixins'

Steps

  1. Start defrosting the shrimpies! It doesn't take as long to thaw as land meats, but get it goin'.
  2. Take your cole slaw mix and toss in some yogurt-based mayonaise for creaminess.
  3. Once your shrimp is all warmed up, season them shits with taco seasoning.
  4. Put them on an oiled-up pan on the hot stove.
  5. Once cooked enough, you can have your tortilla warmed up or cold, just make the dang taco you stupid bastard!
